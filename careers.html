Your Journey

The Path Forward

CoastJet offers a structured progression for every pilot. Whether you're just starting out or ready to take command, there's a seat waiting for you.

01

Second Officer

Your entry point into scheduled operations. You'll fly alongside experienced crew, build procedural discipline, and get comfortable on the line. Most new hires start here.

Entry Level · Open Now
02

First Officer

Progress into more involved operations, supporting Captains and handling increasingly complex routes with greater autonomy and responsibility.

Mid-Level · As We Grow
03

Captain

Take command of your aircraft and lead flights across the network. Ensure safe, efficient operations while mentoring the crew below you.
