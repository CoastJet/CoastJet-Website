About Us

Devon's Own Airline

CoastJet was founded with one idea: Devon deserves better connections to the world. We're a virtual airline based at Exeter International, operating scheduled routes across Europe with a crew that's passionate about aviation and proud of where they come from.

We fly on nSky - a realistic flight simulation network - giving our pilots a structured, professional experience that mirrors real airline operations. Whether it's a short hop to Dublin or a longer sector across the continent, every CoastJet flight is operated to standard.
